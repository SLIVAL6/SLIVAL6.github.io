Armless Samurai

Development Period July 2023 - Present
Project Links
Relevant Skills
Collaborators
Awards 2023 ActivateUTS Club Awards - Initiative of the Year

Armless Samurai is a single-player fast-paced combat game where you play as a samurai with no arms. Dash, kick and fight your way through a seemingly endless fortress with fresh and dynamic combat on your mission to seek vengeance. Face off against numerous monstrous machines and swiftly manoeuvre past tricky obstacles to ascend the fortress to fulfill your vengeful mission.

This game was created as part of the yearly Playmakers Dev Team project, where students work together on a game over the course of a semester. This year, there were over 20 students participating in the development of Armless Samurai. The workflow simulates that of a small game development studio, with students split into smaller department teams, such as programming, art, music, marketing and design. Development took place over several sprints, with weekly sprint meetings using the Agile framework.

My role in this project was as a game designer. I was part of the design team for this project. The most notable things that I was responsible for were:

  • Level design
  • GDD and documentation
  • Design of mechanics


Armless Samurai is still in development and is aiming to release on Steam in early 2024.
